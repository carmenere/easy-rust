# Operators
## Arithmetic operators
- ``+`` addition
- ``-`` subtraction
- ``*``	multiplication
- ``/``	division
- ``%``	modulus

<br>

## Comparison operators

- ``<``
- ``<=``
- ``>``
- ``>=``
- ``==``
- ``!=``

<br>

## Logical operators:
- ``&&`` **AND**
- ``||`` **OR**
- ``!`` **NOT**

<br>

## Bitwise operators (bit level logic):
- ``&`` Bitwise **AND**
- ``|`` Bitwise **OR**
- ``^`` Bitwise **XOR**
- ``!`` Bitwise **NOT**
- ``<<`` Left shift
- ``>>`` Right shift
